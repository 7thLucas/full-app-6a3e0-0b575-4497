AI Career Coach 

The AI Career Coach should proactively monitor each user's career progress and send personalized recommendations and reminders via email to help them stay on track toward their career goals.

Instead of sending generic reminders, the AI should analyze the user's profile, activity, career roadmap, and progress to determine the most relevant guidance.

Examples of AI-generated notifications include:

* Reminder to update a resume after a period of inactivity
* Suggestions to learn a new skill based on the user's target role
* Recommendations for certifications that match the user's career goals
* Interview preparation reminders before upcoming interviews
* Personalized mock interview invitations
* Weekly or monthly career progress summaries
* Job application reminders when users have not applied for jobs recently
* Recommendations to improve ATS scores or strengthen resumes
* Milestone celebrations when users complete learning goals or career objectives
* Personalized career tips and market insights

Users should have full control over their notifications through a dedicated Notification Preferences page. They can enable or disable individual notification types, including:

* Resume reminders
* Learning recommendations
* Interview reminders
* Job application reminders
* Career progress summaries
* AI career tips
* Achievement notifications
* Weekly digest
* Monthly digest

Users should also be able to choose:

* Email frequency (Instant, Daily, Weekly, Monthly)
* Preferred notification time
* Preferred days to receive scheduled emails

The goal is for the AI Career Coach to behave like a proactive mentor that checks in regularly with personalized advice while respecting each user's notification preferences and communication frequency.
