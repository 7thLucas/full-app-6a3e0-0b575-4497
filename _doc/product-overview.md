# AI Career Coach — Product Overview

## Product Identity
- **Name**: AI Career Coach
- **Tagline**: Your always-on career mentor
- **Category**: AI-powered career development SaaS platform

## What It Is
AI Career Coach is a proactive, AI-driven career mentorship platform that monitors each user's career journey and delivers deeply personalized email guidance. Rather than generic check-ins, the AI analyzes each user's profile, activity, career roadmap, and progress to generate targeted recommendations — resume reminders, skill suggestions, certification guidance, interview prep, job application nudges, milestone celebrations, and market insights.

## Target Users
- **Job seekers** actively searching for new roles who need structured guidance and momentum
- **Career advancers** looking to move up within their field through new skills and certifications
- **Career changers** transitioning to new industries who need targeted, role-specific advice
- **Recent graduates** building their first career foundation with guidance on next steps

## Core Problem
Career growth stalls when people lack timely, personalized guidance. Most professionals cannot access a dedicated career coach, and generic reminder tools lack individual context. Users fall behind on resume updates, miss skill-building windows, and apply too infrequently — not from lack of motivation, but lack of a proactive mentor who understands their specific situation.

## The Solution
AI Career Coach acts as a proactive digital mentor that:
1. Continuously monitors user career data (profile, activity, roadmap, progress)
2. Determines the most relevant guidance for each user at each moment
3. Delivers personalized emails with specific, actionable recommendations
4. Respects each user's communication preferences, frequency settings, and notification schedule

## Core AI-Generated Notification Types
1. **Resume update reminders** — triggered after periods of inactivity
2. **Skill learning suggestions** — based on the user's target role
3. **Certification recommendations** — matched to the user's career goals
4. **Interview preparation reminders** — timed before upcoming interviews
5. **Personalized mock interview invitations** — proactively offered
6. **Weekly / monthly career progress summaries** — AI-synthesized career recaps
7. **Job application reminders** — triggered when application activity drops
8. **ATS score improvement recommendations** — to strengthen resumes
9. **Milestone celebrations** — when users complete learning goals or career objectives
10. **Career tips and market insights** — personalized to the user's field

## Notification Preferences System
Users control all communications via a dedicated **Notification Preferences page**.

### Individual Notification Toggles (enable/disable per type)
- Resume reminders
- Learning recommendations
- Interview reminders
- Job application reminders
- Career progress summaries
- AI career tips
- Achievement notifications
- Weekly digest
- Monthly digest

### Scheduling Controls
- **Email frequency**: Instant, Daily, Weekly, Monthly
- **Preferred notification time**: User-selected time of day
- **Preferred days**: Specific days of the week to receive scheduled emails

## Brand & Tone
- **Tone**: Proactive, encouraging, professional — like a trusted mentor who checks in at exactly the right moment
- **Positioning**: The always-on career coach that most professionals can never afford in person
- **Differentiation**: Personalization driven by real user behavior and career data vs. generic reminder tools; full user control over all communication preferences

## Strategic Principles
1. **Personalization over volume** — every email must be relevant to that specific user's current situation
2. **Respecting attention** — user notification preferences are sacred; the system never overrides them
3. **Action-oriented** — every communication drives a specific, achievable career action
4. **Milestone momentum** — celebrate progress consistently to sustain engagement and motivation
5. **User autonomy** — full control over type, frequency, timing, and days of all communications
